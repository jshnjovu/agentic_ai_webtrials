THE BEST BITS

LOCATION

Find us off Broadwick Street, around the corner from bustling Carnaby Street and just 5 minutes from Oxford Circus & Piccadilly Circus Tube stops.

GYM SPACE

An open plan area, equipped with cardio, resistance, functional, free weights and plate-loaded equipment to suit your goals and fitness needs.

3 STUDIOS

Dedicated HIIT Lab, state-of-the-art Spin Studio, and spacious Group Fitness Studio. All weekly classes are included in your membership.

NORDIC SAUNAS

Freshen up after a sweat session in our luxury changing facilities with private access to relaxing Nordic saunas, the perfect place for muscle recovery.

ADD-ONS

Customise your membership to include various add-ons including commuter bike storage, 72hr advance class booking access, locker hire, towel hire, and more.

PROTEIN SHAKE BAR

Pre and post-workout shakes for targeted nourishment are conveniently available next to reception and blended fresh on-site just for you.

COMMUTER CYCLE STORAGE

We understand the needs of London's busy commuters. That's why we've designed a dedicated in-gym cycle storage facility that's secure, convenient, and equipped with everything you need to make your cycling commute stress-free.

THE BLUEPRINT

RECEPTION
Need support? One of our onsite team will assist you with membership or fitness queries whilst you access the club with our biometric fingerprint scanners or via the mobile App.
CYCLE STORAGE
Cycle to work? Customise your membership to include unmonitored or monitored secure bike storage, and keep your bike in tip-top shape at our in-club Bike Repair zone.
HIIT LAB & PT ZONE
Train like a pro with small-group sessions in our dedicated HIIT Lab. Classes integrate high-intensity interval training, functional fitness, athletic training, and agility drills.
SPIN & GROUP FITNESS
Take your fitness to the next level in our Spin Studio equipped with the latest indoor cycling bikes and atmospheric lighting. Our Group Fitness Studio offers a complete range of classes from high-energy Les Mills to relaxing Yoga.
CARDIO & RESISTANCE AREAS
We offer treadmills, cross trainers, upright and recumbent bikes, rowers and more – all designed to help you build endurance, improve your heart and lung fitness, and gain muscle strength.
PLATE LOADED EQUIPMENT
A safe environment for those wanting to lift and train with heavier weights.
FREE WEIGHTS
A comprehensive range of dumbbells, barbells, and functional training methods are provided to improve form and overall strength.

EXPERT SUPPORT

Reach a specific goal, perfect your technique, or simply get a little extra guidance with the help of our talented team of fitness instructors & Personal Trainers.

CLASSES

HIIT

High-intensity interval training sessions combine cardio, strength & agility with weekly programmes to keep you on track and training to your ultimate level.

SPIN

Get on your bike! Combine resistance and cardio-based training in perfect harmony to get your heart pumping, legs burning and metabolism boosted.

YOGA

Increase strength and flexibility, and boost physical and mental well-being through special postures and focused breathing techniques.
